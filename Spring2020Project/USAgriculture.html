Farmers protection

The US government actively protects its farmers. The corona virus pandemic has forced some American farmers to plough their produce and the government is stepping in to compensate them. Most farmers take insurance against any losses.

Large tracts on land

Most farms in US cover thousands on acres. 160 acres is the smallest piece of land a farmer is expected to own. Availability of modern machinery makes it easier for farmers to manage big farms.

Ready market

The US farmers produce a lot of food, for local use and export. There is ready market for grain which is used to produce ethanol for the purposes of mixing with petroleum products. The biggest market for American soy is China.
