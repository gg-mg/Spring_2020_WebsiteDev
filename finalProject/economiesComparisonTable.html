Agricultural economies

Both the US and Kenya are agricultural based econonies but for very different reasons. The US imports most of manufacured goods because the cost of labor makes overseas manufacturing cheaper and more viable. This has left the gap for farming to be the only sector of production that thrives on the US soil. For Kenya, the manufacturing sector is not developed. Kenya imports manufactured goods because the country lacks the technical expertise to manufacture. The Kenyan population grows food to feed itself; its is more out of need rather than as a commercial venture.
