Mechanisation of Agriculture

Machinery Use for Farming in the US

Farming in the U.S is highly mechanised. Most of the agricultural production is handled using machines which are manufactured to carry out specialized tasks.

Farming by hand in Kenya

Most of the farming in Kenya is subsistence farming. Subsistence farms usually consist of no more than a few acres, and farming is done by hand which is very unreliable and of low yield.

Low Level Mechanisation

In the instances of commercial farming in Kenya, the land in use is a few hundred acres with inefficient mechanisation practices which lead to poor crop production. The specialised machinery is not available in the country or is too expensive to import. There is not trained personnel within the country to work with and maintain such specialized equipment.
